Licht — Wasser — Leben

After 15 exciting years in Munich and Cologne, we took the plunge: out of the big city, into life in a small town. Join me on a little journey through my wonderful hometown and gather some inspiration for your real life visit in Lippstadt.

lippstadt focuses on sustainability

The natural environment of Lippstadt is characterized by the Lippe floodplain, numerous flowing waters and quarry ponds as well as the old cultural landscape of the Hellwegbörde. The preservation and development of this special nature and landscape are among the important tasks of sustainable urban development.

Lippstadt calling! Shopping Tips

When you come to Lippstadt to shop, you will find (almost) everything that is also available in the big city: In addition to the large fashion chains, it is mainly long-established retailers that have their place here, as well as interesting boutiques with an unusual range of products.
